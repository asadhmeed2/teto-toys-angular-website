How it started

Our Story

Teto Toys started in a small workshop in 2019 when our founder couldn't find the exact toy parts he needed for a custom build. So he made them himself — and posted pictures online. The response was overwhelming.

Within a year we had partnered with six independent designers and launched our first modular product line. Today we ship worldwide, but our obsession with detail and craftsmanship hasn't changed one bit.
